Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains what information we collect when you use our website and contact us, and how we use it. We keep it simple and we keep your data to a minimum.

What we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect the details you choose to give us:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your business name and type (optional)
  • The message you write to us

We do not use advertising trackers or sell data. Our website does not set marketing cookies.

How we use it

  • To reply to your enquiry and discuss working together.
  • To follow up about the service you asked about.

We will not use your details for anything else, and we will not share them with third parties for their own marketing.

How your message reaches us

Our contact form is delivered through a third-party form service (Web3Forms), which passes your message to our email inbox. Your details are processed only to deliver that message.

Analytics

We use privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics to count visits and see which pages are popular. It does not use cookies, does not track you across other websites, and does not collect information that identifies you personally.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry emails for as long as needed to help you, and remove them when they are no longer relevant. If you'd like your details deleted sooner, just ask.

Your rights

You can ask us to show you, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you at any time. To do so, contact us using the details below.
